VRcollab STUDIO

C.A.V.E ENTERPRISE SOFTWARE

VRcollab STUDIO is a software that allows for quick automated BIM to Stereoscopic Conversion. Enabling C.A.V.E. hardware setups to be turned into BIM Coordination facilities, supporting up to 6 screens and 15 stakeholders.

Participants wear 3D glasses instead of VR Head Mounted Displays (HMD) in order to be immersed in the BIM Design while viewing the stereoscopic projections on multiple screens. This has one advantage over VR HMDs as one participant is not isolated from the other while still being in the virtual environment during coordination.
